# Aerospace Engineering Student  
## CubeSat Systems & Earth Observation

I am an Aerospace Engineering graduate with a strong interest in satellite systems and Earth Observation.  
This website presents a personal engineering project focused on the preliminary design of a CubeSat mission,  
developed to simulate real Phase 0 / Phase A activities in the space industry.

---

## What you'll find here

- A complete CubeSat Earth Observation mission design  
- System-level trade-offs and requirements-driven design  
- Subsystem budgets and performance analysis  
- Python-based modelling and simulation  

---

## Featured Project

**Preliminary Design of a 12U CubeSat for Earth Observation**

The project covers mission requirements definition, orbit selection,  
payload performance analysis, mass and power budgets, ADCS concept,  
and data downlink estimation.
