# Projects

This page presents personal engineering projects developed to simulate real
satellite mission design activities, with a focus on **Systems Engineering**
and **Earth Observation**.

---

## Preliminary Design of a 12U CubeSat for Earth Observation

### Project Overview
This project focuses on the preliminary design (Phase 0 / Phase A) of a 12U CubeSat
dedicated to Earth Observation applications such as agricultural monitoring and
environmental change detection.

The goal of the project is to reproduce a realistic system-level design workflow,
starting from mission requirements and progressing through orbit selection,
payload performance analysis, and subsystem budgeting.

### Engineering Scope
The project covers the following aspects:

- Mission requirements definition
- Orbit selection and coverage analysis (LEO Sun-Synchronous Orbit)
- Payload performance estimation
- Mass and power budget definition with margins
- Preliminary ADCS concept and pointing requirements
- Data downlink estimation and ground segment considerations
